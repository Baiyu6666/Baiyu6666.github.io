Research Interests

Safe Reinforcement Learning

Reinforcement Learning (RL) has shown exceptional successes in a variety of domains like Go, video Games and robotics, but its application in real world is still limited due to lack of safety constraints. We aim to develop safe RL algorithms with explicit safety constraints, promoting the RL in the real world rather than only simulators and games.


Robot control via safe RL. See full video here.

Autonomous Driving

Autonomous driving vehicles are expected to have a huge positive impact on the automotive industry, e.g., to enhance road safety, ease road congestion, decrease fuel consumption and free the human drivers. We are especially interested in applying our safe RL algorithms in decision-making and control of the autonomous vehicles.


End-to-end autonomous driving via RL. See full video here.

Multi-Robot Distributed Control

The use of autonomous mobile robots is a breakthrough for supply chain management, capable of replacing human labor for demanding, repetitive tasks. The key challenge is the control of multiple robots interreacting with each other. We aim to apply the safe RL and also model predictive control to enable multi-robot distributed control.
